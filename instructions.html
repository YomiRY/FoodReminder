Welcome

With Food Reminder you can easily keep track of your foods expirations. Read this short guide to know how to best use the application.

Adding a product

To add a product, simply tap the Add product button and insert a name and an expiration date. You can create more than one product at once by by tapping Save & New.

Deleting a product

Once you have consumed a product, you should remove it from the list. Simply swap your finger over its name to delete it.
